Crystals Collector!

At the start a random number will be provided

The values of the crystals are hidden. Click on them to determine their values. Everytime you click on a crystal its value is added to your total.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number; you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game starts, a new value is assigned to the crystals.
